Design Inspiration
Inspiration: Website link: Reason 01: Reason 02:
Insiration 2 OSRS Wikipedia Excellent ability to transfer information to the user easily using images. Useability, readability, and excellent hyperlinking between pages. Do more wiki edits and study their templates
Insiration 2 OpenRSC Wikipedia I edit this wiki as a side project. It was a great way for me to study organization of information. This project also helped me get better at formatting tables and images for websites. My goal is to expand this wiki project in my spare time.
Insiration 3 Git Hub I love their excellent menuing system, with important account stuff on top and their navigation on the left menu Only pertinent information to the current page is show in nav/manu bars for that page. The crazy amount of settings never take more than a few seconds to find the correct one. Good example of not burying settings.
Insiration 4 Old School Runescape The osrs developers have delivered a retro UI that stayed true to the original design mentality from over 15 years ago. They have successfully added a ton of backend features and useability and quality assurance without changing the retro appeal to the userbase This is a good example of finding a classic design and sticking to it, but improving upon it.
Insiration 5 Steam UI Despite being an application, steam is actually built on top of an in-built web browser (the steam browser). They have delivered a consistently good and easy to use UI for the past 10-15 years with miniml changes and no disruption to user work flow Many other applications and websites radically change the user experience and cause a bad time for all parties.
